PGC Quiz Time Killer

A specialized bookmarklet designed to halt background timers and interval loops on portal scripts.

Drag and drop the button below into your Bookmarks Bar:

🛑 PGC Quiz Time Killer

How to Setup & Run

  1. Make sure your browser's Bookmarks Bar is visible (Ctrl+Shift+B or Cmd+Shift+B).
  2. Drag the red PGC Quiz Time Killer button directly up into the bar.
  3. Open your quiz portal page.
  4. Click the bookmarklet from your bar to stop the active timer loops instantly.
